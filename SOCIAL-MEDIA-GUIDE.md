# ECMWave Social Media Guide

This guide covers setting up Meta Business Suite, using Canva Pro for scheduling, and content templates for ECMWave.

---

## Part 1: Meta Business Suite Setup

### Step 1: Create/Claim Your Facebook Business Page

1. Go to [facebook.com/pages/create](https://facebook.com/pages/create)
2. Choose "Business or Brand"
3. Enter:
   - **Page Name:** Emerald Coast Marketing Wave
   - **Category:** Marketing Agency or Advertising/Marketing
   - **Description:** Shared 9x12 postcard marketing for local businesses in Niceville, Navarre, and Gulf Breeze. Reach 5,000 households with QR-tracked direct mail.

4. Add profile picture (your ECMWave logo)
5. Add cover photo (postcard mockup or Emerald Coast imagery)

### Step 2: Access Meta Business Suite

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Log in with your Facebook account
3. Click "Create Account" if you don't have one
4. Add your ECMWave Facebook Page to your Business Suite

### Step 3: Set Up Posting

1. In Business Suite, click **"Planner"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"Create Post"**
3. Choose platforms (Facebook, Instagram if connected)
4. Write your post, add media
5. Click the dropdown next to "Publish" and select **"Schedule"**
6. Pick your date and time
7. Click **"Schedule"**

### Best Posting Times for Local Business (Florida)

| Day | Best Times |
|-----|------------|
| Tuesday | 9 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM |
| Wednesday | 9 AM, 1 PM |
| Thursday | 9 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM |
| Friday | 9 AM |

Avoid weekends for B2B content; weekends work better for consumer-focused posts.

## Part 2: Canva Content Planner

Your Canva Pro subscription includes social media scheduling.

### Setup

1. Open Canva and go to **canva.com/content-planner**
2. Click **"Connect"** next to Facebook
3. Authorize access to your ECMWave page
4. Repeat for Instagram if desired

### Creating & Scheduling Posts

1. Design your post in Canva (use templates for consistency)
2. Click **"Share"** ‚Üí **"Schedule"**
3. Select your connected Facebook page
4. Choose date and time
5. Click **"Schedule"**

### Pro Tips

- **Create a Brand Kit** in Canva with ECMWave colors:
  - Primary: #059669 (Emerald Green)
  - Accent: #10b981 (Light Emerald)
  - Dark: #1e293b (Slate)

- **Use Templates:** Create 3-4 post templates you can reuse:
  - "New Mailing Announcement"
  - "Spots Available"
  - "Success Story/Testimonial"
  - "Educational/Tips"
